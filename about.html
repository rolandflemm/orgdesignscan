The methodology

Org Topologies™

Org Topologies is a research-based framework that maps how organizations are structured and how that structure either enables or blocks value delivery. It provides a shared language for leaders, teams, and coaches to diagnose organizational patterns — without resorting to consulting jargon.

The MADE method

Map how work flows from idea to customer.
Assess where teams sit on the value spectrum.
Design the org structure that fits your goals.
Elevate with concrete experiments and principles.

Anti-pattern detection

Rather than prescribing a single "right" structure, Org Topologies identifies the patterns that consistently block organizations — coordination overhead, fragmented ownership, specialist bottlenecks — and gives teams the tools to address them.

What is an on-site scan?

The on-site AI-readiness scan goes much deeper than my online free survey. I spend one day at your office to map, assess, and redesign the parts of your organization that matter most. This includes talking to the people who do the work, observing some meetings and discussing the findings with the leadership team.

1

Map your value streams

I draw the full picture of how work flows in your organization — from the first idea to the customer receiving value.

2

Assess your topology

I place every significant team and work unit on the map. Patterns — good and bad — become immediately visible.

3

Design and elevate

Together, I redesign the structure and define a set of experiments your teams can start the following Monday.
