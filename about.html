The methodology

Org Topologies™

Org Topologies is a research-based framework that maps how organizations are structured and how that structure either enables or blocks value delivery. It provides a shared language for leaders, teams, and coaches to diagnose organizational patterns — without resorting to consulting jargon.

The MADE method

Map how work flows from idea to customer.
Assess where teams sit on the value spectrum.
Design the org structure that fits your goals.
Elevate with concrete experiments and principles.

Anti-pattern detection

Rather than prescribing a single "right" structure, Org Topologies identifies the patterns that consistently block organizations — coordination overhead, fragmented ownership, specialist bottlenecks — and gives teams the tools to address them.
